WELCOME TO TUKULE LUNCH

This is a web application that helps the Main Company Seventh Day Adventist Church (Nairobi) to raise funds for their Sabbath lunch kitty.
It targets all church members and well wishers to pledge the available items and commit to the same.

HOW TO PLEDGE AND COMMIT

  1. Select the category of items you wish to pledge from the CATEGORIES dropdown menu in the navbar e.g Pledge Food Items
  2. Select a specific item within your preferred category and click on it to display more information
    including the Pledge This Item button
  3. Click on the Pledge This Item button, this will lead you to a summary of your pledge(s)
  4. You can increase the quantity of the pledged item by pressing the button
    Or reduce the quantity using the button
    Or delete a pledged item using the button.
  5. You can pledge more items by pressing the Pledge More Items button. You will see the item added and the total cash increasing too
  6. Once you have reviewed your pledged items and desire to commit to them, press the COMMIT button
  7. Fill the Committment Form with accurate details as required then press the SUBMIT button
  8. You will then receive an email to the email address you provided in the commitment form
    It contains all the items you have committed to and the corresponding total amount of cash.
  9. In order to track your commitment and payment histories you will need to create an account by signing up or signing in
  10. If you need to sign up, the ensure you provide the same email that you used to commit in the commitment form
  11. The Payment details are in the FOOTER

THANK YOU AND MAY GOD BLESS YOU FOR TAKING TIME TO PLEDGE AND COMMIT!
